Approach

Binance came up with an innovative product - Binance Pay, it is the world's first system that allows accepting payments in cryptocurrency for physical goods, for example, for buying food, for gasoline at gas stations and petrol stations.

The company also decided to develop a single digital wallet that would allow it to store a balance and use it to pay at various partner locations/sites. Such products were Binance Pay and Binance Settle Wallet.

Solution

We developed the company's digital brand strategy and designed an innovative ux/ui interface design for web and mobile apps for the Binance pay system, wallet, and integrated products.
